WHAT IS FITNESS?

Fitness refers to a state of health and well-being, achieved through regular physical activity, proper nutrition, and adequate rest. It encompasses various components such as cardiovascular endurance, muscular strength, flexibility, and body composition. Being fit helps improve overall quality of life, boosts energy levels, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.

HOW TO MAKE DANGEROUS BICEPS AND TRICEPS

To achieve impressive biceps and triceps, focus on a combination of strength training exercises, proper nutrition, and adequate rest. Here are some effective exercises:

  • Bicep Curls: Use dumbbells or a barbell to perform curls, focusing on controlled movements.
  • Tricep Dips: Use parallel bars or a sturdy bench to perform dips, targeting the triceps effectively.
  • Hammer Curls: Hold dumbbells with palms facing each other to work both biceps and forearms.
  • Skull Crushers: Lie on a bench and lower a barbell or dumbbells towards your forehead, then extend back up.
  • Close-Grip Bench Press: A variation of the bench press that emphasizes the triceps.
